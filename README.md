## About the Project

**BOIDE (Business Optimization Intelligence & Decision Engine)** is an AI-powered data-driven decision support system that transforms raw business data into meaningful insights and actionable recommendations.

The system analyzes key business operations such as revenue trends, customer behavior, and delivery performance to support effective decision-making in e-commerce analytics.

---

## Key Features

- **KPI Computation** - Calculates and tracks key business performance indicators
- **Time Series Forecasting** - Uses ARIMA model to predict future business trends
- **Customer Segmentation** - K-Means clustering to identify customer behavior patterns
- **Anomaly Detection** - Statistical Z-score methods to detect irregularities
- **Rule-based Decision Engine** - Converts insights into practical business recommendations
- **Interactive Dashboard** - Clear visualization for easy interpretation
- **Digital Twin Simulation** - Simulates real-world business scenarios
- **Explainable AI** - Transparent and understandable AI recommendations
